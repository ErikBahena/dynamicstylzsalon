Good to know

Questions, answered. Frequently asked questions about Dynamic Stylz Salon in Elma, WA.

The short versions — text Amber at (360) 581-2428 for anything else.

Where is Dynamic Stylz Salon located?

Dynamic Stylz Salon is at 313 W Main St in downtown Elma, WA — the third door down from Dollar Tree on Main Street. Free alley parking is behind the building. We serve Elma, Montesano, McCleary, Satsop, and the greater Grays Harbor County area.

What are your hours?

We are open Monday through Thursday 10am–5pm and Friday 10am–4pm. We are closed Saturday and Sunday. Call or text (360) 581-2428 to book.

Do you accept walk-ins?

Walk-ins are welcome whenever a stylist is available, but appointments are preferred — especially for color, balayage, and perms. Text (360) 581-2428 and we will find the next open chair.

How much does a haircut cost?

Pricing varies by length, density, and time in the chair. A free consultation is always the first step — text Amber for a quote tailored to your hair.

Do you do balayage and highlights?

Yes. Amber specializes in dimensional, lived-in color — hand-painted balayage, traditional foiled highlights, all-over color, and gentle gray blending. A short consultation is always free so we can match the technique to your hair.

Do you offer sensory-friendly appointments?

Yes. Mention it when you book and we will dim the lights, skip the small talk, and pace the appointment around what you need. Kids and adults both welcome.

Do you cut kids' hair?

Absolutely — first haircuts are a specialty. We keep the appointment quick, friendly, and as fun as possible, with sensory-friendly accommodations available on request.

What forms of payment do you accept?

We take cash, debit, and all major credit cards.

How do I book an appointment?

The fastest way is to text or call (360) 581-2428. You can also message us on Facebook and we will get back to you the same business day.
