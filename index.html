Meet your stylist

Amber Aho — owner & stylist.

Amber has spent more than two decades turning small-town chairs into safe, welcoming spaces. She built Dynamic Stylz as a place where grandparents, teenagers, toddlers, and everyone in between can sit down, be listened to, and leave feeling a little more like themselves.

Quiet appointments are available on request — perfect for sensory sensitivities or anyone who just wants a calmer visit. Free consultations are always included, because the best cut starts with a good conversation.
